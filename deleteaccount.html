Delete Your Account - Atomic Calories

If you wish to delete your account and all related data from the Atomic Calories app, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to yourturnai@gmail.com with the subject line: “Delete My Account”.

In your message, please include the email address you used to register in the app.

Your request will be processed within 7 business days, and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Account information (email, profile details)
  • Personal usage history and logs
  • Any messages or inputs provided within the app

Data That May Be Retained (If Applicable)

  • Payment records or transaction data (stored securely, for legal compliance, retained up to 12 months)

Additional Information

If you no longer use Atomic Calories, you can also remove the app from your device. For full data removal, please request deletion as described above.
